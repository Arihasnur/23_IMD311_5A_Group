About Us

guppies for customers to choose from. This company has been operating since 2022. Guppies are easier in terms of
reproduction because guppies give birth instead of laying eggs. Our guppy fish are widely supplied through
domestic and foreign markets. Every business of buying and selling from this company will be managed by staff from
their respective departments, whether from online or walk-in sales. Our company attaches great importance to good
customer service. Because of this our company became a well-known supplier company to be used as the main
